Krave Burger

Overview

Cuisine: American, Canadian cuisine, vegetarian friendly and with gluten free options.

Price: $10 - $20

Fast food restaurant. Singular American burgers with generous beef and tasty poutine potatoes. They also have other options such as chicken sandwiches and chicken tenders on their varied menu.
