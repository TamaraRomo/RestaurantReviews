Mucho Burrito

Overview

Cuisine: Mexican-inspired cuisine, vegetarian friendly.

Price: $10 - $20

Mexican fast food restaurant. Food free of artificial preservatives, fake flavours, and artificial fat substitutes. Including all-natural salsas made with 100% real and fresh ingredients and traditional fire-roasted cooking techniques.
